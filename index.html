100% Free, Forever

Talent is equally distributed. Opportunity is not. There are kids in small towns, rural areas, and underfunded schools who are brilliant — who could build spacecraft and design robots — but nobody ever showed them how. Nobody told them they could. Launch Lab exists to close that gap. Every guide on this site is free. Every tool we recommend is free. Every project uses parts you can buy with lawn-mowing money. We are not a nonprofit looking for donations. We are a garage full of kids who refused to wait for permission.

I built my first project in a garage with $30 of parts. That's all it took.
